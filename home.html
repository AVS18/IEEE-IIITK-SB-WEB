IEEE Student Branch IIIT Kottayam

   Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kottayam at Valavoor, Pala, Kerala is one among the IIITs that have been established as "Institutions of National Importance" by MHRD Govt. of India under the ambit of IIIT (PPP) Act 2017.


   IEEE-IIITK is an IEEE initiation started at our institute to enrich the technical activities of our students/faculty.


    IEEE Student Branch at IIIT Kottayam has been approved by IEEE USA in 2020 and comes under IEEE Kerala Section, India (R-10 Region).

Vision and Mission

   IEEE will be essential to the global technical community and to technical professionals everywhere, and be universally recognized for the contributions of technology and of technical professionals in improving global conditions

   IEEE's core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.
